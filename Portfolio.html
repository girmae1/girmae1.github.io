Hand drawn Friendship Flowers

My Friendship Flowers are adapted from Giorgia Lupi's Week of Friends. This visualization shows my close friends and my relationship with them. For example, I show the topics we talk about, how long I've known them for and how often we talk.

Friendship Networks Using Graph Theory

I have 2 friendship networks. My first network shows my relationship to everyone in my friend group and their relationships to each other as well. My second network is of k-dramas I have watched and my friends who watch k-dramas. By doing this, I can check if any of the k-dramas I've watched are an indicator of a relationship.

An Ode to K-drama Buddies: Hand drawn K-drama Flowers

My hand drawn K-drama flowers are adapted from Giorgia Lupi and Stefanie Posavec's Ode to Friendship from their book Observe, Collect, Draw! Each k-drama I've watched is one flower, and I cover attributes such my enjoyment of the k-drama, the friends I've watched it with.

Korean Drama Flowers

This is adpated from Shirley Wu's Film Flowers. I visualize the k-dramas I've watched as flowers. I use data from imdb to code for the flower's petal size, number of petals and color of the flower.
