Welcome to My Website!

My name is Ellena. I am working on my Senior Capstone Project and I thought a website would be the perfect platform to share my work. My goal for this capstone project was to have it be reflective of the many disciplines that interest me. I've always wanted to find creative ways to tell stories and learn new tools. So I took on a capstone project that would allow me to share my experiences as an international student and teach me how to visualize data from my life. There are three main areas that I wanted to explore with this project:

  1. Communication
  2. Programming
  3. Digital Art and Visual works

I picked these disciplines because they focus on utilizing storytelling techniques to allow an audience to interact with a creator's work in multiple ways.
